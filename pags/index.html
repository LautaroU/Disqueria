Illmatic (1994)
Nas
If you're reading this, you probably shouldn't; drop everything and go listen to this amazing record. When you hear people say that it's the best hip-hop album of all time, it's not an euphemism or an exaggeration, it really is, and the acclaims are purposefully awarded to this timeless piece of music, and believe me that you will agree with me once you end your listening session with 'It Ain't Hard To Tell'. You'll come back to this album. Maybe more than one time, maybe hundreds like I do, I can't get enough of it.

-Sapleado, 2022.
Graduation (2007)
Kanye West
Kanye starts the album with 'Good Morning', showing us his new fresh sound. Synthesizers, new samples and influnce from others genders, wich makes this album a materpice from his repertory. I went crazy the first time a heard 'Flashing Light', that influnce from House Music blow my mind in the first minute from listening the song. It was a complete new sound for West and he shows us what he will be able to do in his next albums. I absolutely recommend this album.
